About : Smart Contact Manager

Welcome to Smart Contact Manager, your one-stop solution for managing your contacts effortlessly on the cloud. Our platform provides you with the tools you need to keep your contacts organized, up-to-date, and accessible from anywhere.

## Mission
Our mission is to simplify contact management for individuals and businesses, ensuring that you never lose track of important connections. We believe that an organized contact list can save you time, enhance your professional relationships, and boost your productivity.

## Key Features
-> Cloud Storage: Store your contacts securely in the cloud, accessible from any device with an internet connection.
-> Add, Edit, and Delete: Easily add new contacts, update existing ones, and remove outdated information.
-> Search and Filter: Quickly find the contacts you need using powerful search and filtering options.
-> Collaboration: Share contact lists with colleagues or family members, enabling seamless collaboration.
-> Responsive Design: Enjoy a consistent experience across desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
